Damu Management Consult PLC (DAMU) is a consultancy service providing firm established in 2017 to join the consultancy world. Damu is working on establishing concrete ethical values and code of conduct in executing its business activities and interacting with its stakeholders including its employees, management, clients, government organizations and many others. We built different policy manuals for governing our operations to move towards success.

What we do?

Damu Management Consult PLC works on providing consultancy services on different Areas. We conduct studies such as feasibility studies, baseline studies, capacity needs assessment, pre-grant assessments, Value Chain Analysis, and other forms of researches. We also prepare Business Plan, Training and other organizational Manuals. In addition, we providing trainings, develope organizational structure, salary scale and benefits scheme, project evaluations (Mid-term and Final), develope strategic plans.
